Directions

Set your maps to Manchester Airport and exit the M56 at Junction 5 and get in lane for Terminal 1 (T1). At the set of traffic lights, go straight ahead for T1.

At the roundabout, take the 5th exit and follow signs for T1 Meet & Greet (T1 M&G). After 0.1 mile take the first available left turn and continue 0.1mile to a roundabout. Take the first exit and the entrance to T1 M&g is then immediately on your right.

Directions

Approach Manchester Airport and exit the M56 at Junction 5 and get in lane for Terminal 1 (T1). At the set of traffic lights, turn right for T2.

Follow signs for T2 Meet & Greet (T1 M&G). After 0.1 mile take the first available left turn and continue 0.1mile to a roundabout. Take the first exit, then the third, then the second, then the first again and the entrance to T2 is immediately on your right.

Directions

Upon arrival at Manchester Airport exit the M56 at Junction 5 and get in lane for Terminal 1 (T1). At the set of traffic lights, turn right for T3.

Follow signs for T3 Meet & Greet (T3 M&G). After 0.6 mile take the first available left turn and the entrance to T3 is immediately on your right.
